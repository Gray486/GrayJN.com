Infinte Campus GPA Calculator

This is a tool to make caculating your current GPA much easier.

How to use:
1. Open Infinite Campus portal and navigate to "Grades" page and click on the semester.
2. Expand all class rows including GPA, and close the "Settings" panel.
3. Click on the word "Grades" at the top of the page.
4. Click Ctrl+A to highlight the whole page.
5. Click Ctrl+C to copy the whole page.
6. Paste (Ctrl+V) into the box below.
7. Click the "Calculate" button.



¹Class Averages are only based on classes this semester.
²Weighted GPA is EVENLY averaged with current IC GPA and weighted class average.
